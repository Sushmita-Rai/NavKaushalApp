NavKaushal is a mobile application designed to help athletes realize their dreams by providing a digital platform for talent identification and sports guidance. The app allows users to take basic physical tests from the comfort of their home, receive personalized sports recommendations, and stay updated on tournaments happening nearby.


How It Works

Sign Up / Login: Create an account or log in to access the app features.

Take Tests: Perform basic physical tests such as speed, endurance, and agility tests.

Receive Recommendations: Based on test results, get guidance on suitable sports.

Explore Tournaments: Check upcoming tournaments and events in your area.

Get Noticed: Talents can be discovered by sports organizers and coaches.
